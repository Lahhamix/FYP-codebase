\chapter{Full List of Needs}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Patients need rehabilitation programs for gait and balance improvement.
    \item Patients need assistive devices (walkers, wheelchairs, orthotics) at lower cost.
    \item Patients need wearable devices to monitor gait abnormalities.
    \item Patients need pressure-sensing insoles to detect walking changes.
    \item Patients need home-based monitoring systems for early relapse detection.
    \item Patients need telemedicine services to reduce hospital visits.
    \item Patients need data-driven platforms for personalized treatment recommendations.
    \item Patients need low-power wearable sensors that can continuously track fatigue levels through heart rate variability.
    \item Patients need smart insoles capable of detecting asymmetry between the left and right foot during walking.
    \item Patients need motion-capture systems that work affordably with smartphones instead of expensive lab equipment.
    \item Patients need cloud-based dashboards that doctors can access to monitor patient progress remotely.
    \item Patients need voice-controlled systems to log daily symptoms hands-free.
    \item Patients need advanced gyroscope/accelerometer-based fall detection systems.
    \item Patients need electronic medical records that they can access and transfer from clinic to clinic if needed.
    \item Patients need integration of gait monitoring data with electronic medical records in Lebanese hospitals.
    \item Patients need AI-driven anomaly detection that can alert doctors when unusual gait patterns occur.
    \item Patients need low-cost pressure-sensing insoles that can detect early gait abnormalities linked to neurodegenerative disease progression.
    \item Patients need real-time plantar pressure heatmaps to help doctors visualize walking asymmetry and balance issues.
    \item Patients need long-term monitoring of foot pressure during daily activities (not just in clinic) to capture subtle changes.
    \item Patients need machine learning models trained on plantar pressure data to distinguish neuro-degenerative disease-related gait changes from normal variations.
    \item Patients with neuro-degenerative-related ataxia need precise foot pressure sensors to detect irregular weight distribution that signals balance loss before a fall occurs.
    \item Patients need foot pressure monitoring to quantify fatigue-related gait deterioration, since subjective reporting alone is unreliable.
    \item Patients need continuous foot pressure data to help doctors track disease progression objectively rather than relying only on periodic clinic visits.
    \item Patients need sensor-based foot pressure analysis to personalize physiotherapy plans, ensuring exercises target their specific gait weaknesses.
    \item Patients with neuro-degenerative disease need foot pressure sensors to provide early warnings of subtle balance deterioration, allowing interventions before mobility loss becomes severe.
    \item Patients need a customized foot pressure sensing platform that is affordable and designed specifically for Lebanon, since commercial systems (e.g., Emed®) are too expensive and inaccessible.
    \item Patients need a wearable plantar pressure system that can be used outside of clinics, enabling continuous monitoring during real-life walking instead of only lab tests.
    \item Patients need machine-learning analysis of foot pressure patterns to detect subtle gait abnormalities that neurologists may not identify through visual observation alone.
    \item Patients need standardized digital gait biomarkers from foot pressure data so doctors can objectively track disease progression over time.
    \item Patients need sensor-based rehabilitation feedback systems, where foot pressure changes are instantly translated into feedback for physiotherapists to improve balance and mobility training.
    \item Patients need multilingual mobile apps (Arabic, English, French) tailored to Lebanese patients for symptom reporting.
    \item Patients need AI research tailored to Lebanese gait data.
    \item Patients need open-access databases for MS research.
    \item Patients need cloud-based health records accessible to all doctors.
    \item Patients need long-term strategies for independent living.
    \item Patients need subsidies for costly medications.
    \item Patients need reduced hospital fees for long-term treatments.
    \item Patients need employment protection laws to secure income.
    \item Patients need financial relief for caregivers who quit jobs.
    \item Patients need mental health support for depression and anxiety.
    \item Patients need hope-based rehabilitation programs.
    \item Patients need government recognition as people with special needs.
    \item Patients need educational resources about MS in Arabic.
    \item Patients need disability-friendly policies in employment.
    \item Patients need better enforcement of disability rights.
    \item Patients need protection from job discrimination.
    \item Patients need ergonomic home furniture.
    \item Patients need home modifications for mobility (ramps, lifts).
    \item Patients need reliable power for medical devices during outages.
    \item Patients need mobility-friendly transportation apps.
\end{enumerate}
