\chapter{Weekly Meeting Minutes}
%write something here
The minutes of the official weekly meetings that were documented during the Final Year Project's progress are compiled in this section. Each meeting was an opportunity to assess ongoing tasks, assign new duties, and address technical or logistical problems that arose during research and preliminary development. The meeting minutes serve as a chronological record of decisions, milestones reached, and team discussions, helping to keep the project transparent and continuously improve the development process.
